Mechanical Energy and Our Mini Lift Project

Mechanical energy is all about how things move and how their position stores energy. It’s made up of two parts: the energy things have when they’re moving (kinetic energy) and the energy they have because of where they are (potential energy). Knowing how this energy moves and changes helps us build machines that work better.


For our project, we decided to make a mini lift using materials like box and straw as a pulley. We used what we learned about mechanical energy to turn the spinning of a handle into lifting something up. It was amazing to see how energy we put in by turning the handle gets changed into movement that lifts objects smoothly and easily.
